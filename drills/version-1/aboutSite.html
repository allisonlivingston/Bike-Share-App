Welcome!

Whether you're on the go or want to spend the day cruising around town, this site is for you! This site provides real time status of bicycles at stations throughout the world, thanks to CityBikes. Need to find a bike to use or a station to drop off your bike? Simply go to our "Find A Bike" page and select your desired country and city to check out the number of bikes and open slots available at each participating bike station in your local area.
